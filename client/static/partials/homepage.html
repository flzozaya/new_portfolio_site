My name is Lorenzo and I am a Software Engineer with a focus in Frontend and over 8 years of industry experience, currently working at Hulu for the past 2+ years.
I enjoy all things tech, but most passionate about web technologies and the marriage between technology and art as an expression in web development.

I was born in Venezuela, but lived most of my life in South Florida, before moving to LA almost 3 years ago. In my spare time, I have fun with cool web experiments, building a product for a client or learning something new.
But I'm more of an outdoor person - I enjoy traveling, hiking, the beach. I also have a weakness for soccer, beach volleyball and ping-pong. And a passion for music - playing guitar since the age of 13.
