more about me

My name is Lorenzo and I am a Front-End Developer. A lot of people unfamilar with the tech term, ask me: Well what does that mean? The meaning of something can either be its literal/textual concept, or its significant purpose someone can understand at a deeper, more meaningful, yet simpler level.

So I say - I help regular people find a way to be more competitive online by creating a web presence for them. And help businesses make the web a better place by building tools businesses and their users can find useful.

I am fully engaged in the marriage between logic and art as an expression of web development. Meticulous, humble and a fast learner. Reflecting a life philosophy around functionality, practicality, simplicity and minimalism, with an appreciation, focus and strive for the logical aspects of digital design.

Somewhere in between of all that, in my spare time, I have fun with cool web experiments, traveling or playing soccer. I have a passion for music - playing guitar since the age of 13 - and a weakness for beach volleyball.
I also enjoy ping-pong and hiking.
